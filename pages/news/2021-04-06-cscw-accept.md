Our paper, "From Archives to Interfaces: Exploring the Potential of Emulation for Software Research, Pedagogy, and Design", 
won a Methods Recognition Award at [Computer Supported Cooperative Work] (CSCW) 2021. This award is given to papers that contain "well developed, explained, or implemented methods, and/or methodological innovation."
