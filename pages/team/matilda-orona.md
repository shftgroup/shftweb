Matilda Orona received a B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics from [California State University Channel Islands]. 

As part of SHFT’s 2021–2022 and 2022–2023 HSI-SMART cohorts, her research examined the impact of Adobe Flash deprecation on web preservation and explored the broader implications of regional software deprecation.

Matilda is currently pursuing a Master of Information and Data Science at the [UC Berkeley School of Information](https://www.ischool.berkeley.edu/).
